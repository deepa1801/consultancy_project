                        Service

Our Motto is “to keep both our buyer and seller happy in every transaction, doing so will ensure our well being.”

This belief and approach towards our business is purely based on our service strength. We understand that in business, retaining existing customers is as crucial as acquiring a new one. To serve better and to retain our customer’s loyalty, we go an extra mile and provide top notch services, global-class quality yarn products and solutions and cater to their needs round the clock, receive their queries, and act on them in a timely manner.

As there are numerous options out there, our proven expertise and decade old experience help us to find the ‘right yarn material’, at the ‘right price’ that would best fit our customer’s business needs. In addition, we also keep track of the entire process – from a thorough research to sourcing the right yarn, to stocking/ warehousing timely, and delivery on time, within budget.

Nowadays, it can broadly be divided into three types. Real zari, imitation zari, and metallic zari. Real Zari is made from fine silver or gold thread is drawn from silver or gold alloys, which is flattened by passing it under equal pressure rotating rollers.
